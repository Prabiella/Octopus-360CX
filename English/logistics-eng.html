NATIONWIDE LOGISTICS SERVICES

With over a decade of experience across various industries, Octopus is the logistics partner that supports you in managing your entire supply chain through the following infrastructure:

  • Distribution centers in Mexico City (CDMX), Guadalajara (GDL), and Monterrey (MTY).
  • Logistics services with a network of 24 warehouses strategically located throughout the country, featuring scalable expansion capabilities.
  • 4,000 m² of storage space.
  • National cross-docking operations.
  • Inventory management and control through Oracle ERP.
  • Designated areas for loading and receiving at service bays.
  • Experienced personnel trained in logistics applications.
  • Partnerships with major carriers: Estafeta, Paquete Express, and DHL.

SERVICES

Our mission is to build trust with our clients, reduce supply chain costs, mitigate risks, and increase operational efficiency. Below are some of our specialized services.

Logistics Consulting & Solutions

We combine our industry expertise with your objectives to deliver value-added logistics solutions that standardize your transportation and logistics processes—from long-term planning to supply chain optimization and cost reduction.

Warehousing

We professionally safeguard your products; guaranteeing security and availability in receiving, distribution, packaging, labeling, quality inspection, and more. CCTV monitoring is available.

Inventory Management

We manage and track goods from their point of origin to our warehouses, and from our facilities to the final delivery location. We maintain the right balance between working capital and required service levels.

Primary Distribution & Transportation Logistics

We evaluate and design optimized logistics routes. We supervise, execute, and manage all transportation and distribution operations.

Order Fulfillment & Integration

Orders can be delivered via service bay, local pickup, or courier.

Custom Kitting & Maquila

Repackaging of damaged goods, re-boxing into new outer cartons, and managing custom packaging per country. We label your products with client-specific information, including logos, barcodes, engraving, stamping, re-labeling, and more.

Reverse Logistics

Return of products through local reception, consolidation, and classification for shipment to the client or our repair center, where we can assist with restoration, refurbishment, recycling, direct reuse, cannibalization, or destruction.
